### How It Works
1. Trade enters at liquidity sweep reversal
2. Price moves favorably and hits 1:1 Risk:Reward
3. Stop Loss automatically moves to entry (breakeven)
4. Trade becomes "free roll" - either hits TP or exits at breakeven
5. No loss possible after 1:1 RR achieved
